## Limitations

- `readdir_plus` is approximated in Python with `os.scandir(...).stat(...)`; actual kernel FUSE callback choice remains kernel-dependent.
- Startup/session timing is measured as wrapper elapsed time minus child workload time, so it is an estimate rather than a direct mount lifecycle probe.
